We believe pickleball is more than just a game; it is a community builder that bridges generations, backgrounds, and skill levels.

Built for All Skill Levels

Whether you are picking up a paddle for the very first time or grinding for a higher DUPR rating, Epic provides a welcoming, structured space for you to grow.

Weather-Proof Athletics

Colorado weather is unpredictable. Our climate-controlled courts ensure your weekly games and training sessions never get rained or snowed out.

Fostering Connections

We heavily emphasize the social aspect of the sport. We host Friday night events, open play mixers, and social leagues designed to help you meet your neighbors.

Experience professional-grade amenities designed specifically for the optimal pickleball experience, not just lines taped onto a converted tennis court.

Professional Grade Surfaces

Our customized court surfaces offer the perfect balance of grip and give. This protects your joints during intense play while ensuring a true, consistent bounce.

Optimized Lighting & Acoustics

We installed specialized LED sports lighting and acoustic paneling. This eliminates blinding glare on lobs and reduces the chaotic echo typically found in warehouse facilities.

Full-Service Pro Shop & Lounge

Demo the latest paddles from top brands, grab a post-match drink at our craft bar, or just relax in our premium viewing lounges while waiting for your next game.

Epic Pickleball Club is built on a foundation of respect, excellent sportsmanship, and a shared love for the fastest-growing sport in America.

The Paddle Tap Policy

We win with grace and we lose with class. At Epic, every match ends with a paddle tap at the net and a genuine smile.

Inclusive Rotations

During our drop-in and open play sessions, our community ensures everyone gets fair time on the court. We rotate consistently and always welcome new faces to the four-some.

Continuous Growth

We encourage our advanced players to mentor those just starting out. We foster an environment where everyone leaves their ego at the door and elevates their game together.
