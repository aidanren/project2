Epic Dink N Drink

  • Format: Friday Night Social
  • Vibe: Casual & High Energy
  • Includes: Pizza and Open Play
RSVP for Friday

Friday Night Pickleball

Kick off your weekend the right way. Dink N Drink is our premier weekly social event combining open play with great food and drinks in a relaxed club atmosphere.

Your ticket includes guaranteed court time in a round-robin rotation, fresh pizza, and access to our full-service craft bar. It is the perfect opportunity to meet new partners and socialize off the court.

Event Details

  • When: Select Friday Nights (check calendar).
  • Who: Open to members and non-members.
  • Bar: Local craft beers, seltzers, and specialty cocktails available.
